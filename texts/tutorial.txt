You are the player that is represented with symbol: @
Empty spaces are represented with symbol: *
And walls are represented with symbol: #
You always start at dungeon entrance: U
And you need to leave dungeon at the exit: I
That's it! You get 1 point for escaping 1 level.
Bonus points are possible.

There will be... inconveniences along your way.
Nothing serious, really. I swear.

Ingame help is available. Just type "help".
